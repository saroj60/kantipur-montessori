The Montessori Approach

"The child is both a hope and a promise for mankind." — Dr. Maria Montessori

Our training is rooted in the belief that children are born with an innate drive to learn. We teach educators how to create a "Prepared Environment" that fosters independence, creativity, and a lifelong love for learning. You'll learn to observe children as scientists of the soul.
