About the Center

Pioneering Excellence in Teacher Education since 2024.

Our Mission & Vision

Kantipur Montessori Teacher training center is a professional training institute dedicated to preparing skilled and confident Montessori teachers. We focus on providing both theoretical knowledge and practical classroom experience based on the principles of Maria Montessori.

visibility

Our Vision

To develop qualified educators who can guide children with care, creativity and modern teaching techniques.

Our Mission

To provide rigorous, accessible, and high-quality Montessori training that blends original philosophy with contemporary science.

“An investment in knowledge pays the best interest” – Benjamin Franklin

Maria Montessori's philosophy is at the core of everything we do. We teach our students to become "scientists of the soul"—observing children with precision and guiding them with love.

Why Choose Kantipur?

What sets us apart from ordinary training centers.

Practical-Based Learning

Emphasis on hands-on training with Montessori materials, not just theory, helping trainees gain real teaching skills.

Experienced Trainers

Qualified and experienced instructors guide students with deep knowledge of Montessori methods.

Child-Centered Approach

Training is focus on understanding each child’s needs, interests, and development stages.

Well-Equipped Environment

The Center provides a prepared Montessori classroom with proper learning materials for practice.

Skill Development

Trainees learn communication skills, classroom management and teaching techniques.

Job Oriented Training

The course is designed to make trainees ready for real teaching jobs in school and Montessori centers.

Supportive Learning Atmosphere

Friendly and encouraging environment helps students build confidence.
